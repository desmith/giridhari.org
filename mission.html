Our Mission (Giridhari's Mission?)

Once a week we invite everyone into our home for the purpose of sharing Krsna consciousness. Living the philosophy of Krsna consciousness throughout the week (following the regulative principles, chanting 16 rounds, rising early, reading scriptures an hour a day, listening to lectures and kirtans whenever possible, eating only Krsna prasad and worshiping Radha Giridhari with regulation and bhakti) we live in a purifying environment. Our lifestyle is an example and inspiration to our guests.

We take the time each week to prepare a meaningful class to give our guests something to take home with them for at least that week. Keeping a small group allows us to treat each person as an individual encouraging them according to their propensity and qualities.

What would we really like to be and do in our program?
  • Facilitate a forum for spiritual growth, inquiry, inspiration
  • Example of one whom is advancing in spiritual life
  • Enliven individuals according to their propensity
  • Convey the teachings of Srila Prabhupada
What do I feel are our greatest strengths?
  • Determination to please Mukunda Goswami
  • Understanding of philosophy according to Srila Prabhupada
  • Practical realizations of the application of Krsna consciousness
  • Compassion
  • Desire to share Krsna consciousness with others
How do I want us to be remembered?
  • Sincere devotees who loved Krsna, Prabhupada, Mukunda Goswami and all devotees
  • We showed our love through living an exemplary life and always made choices to please the Vaisnavas
  • What have been my happiest moments in life?
  • If I had unlimited time and resources, wheat would I do?
  • What are the three or four most important things to me?
