Our Mission

Every week we have a program in our home for the sharing of Krsna consciousness. We strive to "live" the philosophy of Krsna consciousness throughout the week to the best of our ability so that we will have something meaningful to offer to our guests.

The format is simple - we start with a traditional arotik to Their Lordships Sri-Sri Radha Giridhari, during which time we have kirtana (congregational chanting), then there is a class on Vedic philosophy along with a group discussion so that everyone can share their realizations, ask questions, etc. After the class we do our best to make sure everyone is fully satisfied by feeding them a sumputous vegetarian feast.
